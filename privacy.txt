Privacy Policy for Stolpguide

Effective Date: June 30, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Stolpguide, a tool designed to assist locksmiths in finding the right strike plate for electric locks.
This Privacy Policy explains how Stolpguide handles your information when you use our app. By using Stolpguide,
you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Stolpguide is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.
Specifically, Stolpguide does not collect the following information:

    Personal identifiers (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
    Location data
    Device information
    Usage data

3. How Stolpguide Works

Stolpguide operates entirely on your device. The app downloads information from a website to provide a list of strike plates for
electric locks. The search queries you enter are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted, stored, or collected
by Stolpguide.

4. Third-Party Services

Stolpguide may use third-party services to download information from the internet. However, these services are used solely to
fetch data necessary for the app's functionality and do not involve the collection of user data.

5. Security

While Stolpguide does not collect personal information, we are committed to ensuring that any data downloaded to your device
is handled securely. We implement appropriate security measures to protect the information processed by the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Stolpguide is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has used the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
